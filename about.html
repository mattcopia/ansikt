About Ansikt

A Norwegian word for face. A Putney studio built on the belief that beauty should feel calm, personal, and completely yours.

The Founder

Beauty that begins with understanding

Lauren founded Ansikt with a clear conviction: every face tells a story, and great skincare should honour it rather than hide it. Raised with a deep appreciation for Scandinavian simplicity — ansikt is the Norwegian word for face — she brings that same honesty and warmth to every treatment.

With years of experience in bespoke facial and brow treatments, Lauren trained with some of the industry's most respected names before opening her own studio in Putney. Her approach is unhurried and deeply personal — no two appointments are ever the same, because no two faces are.

The products she chooses reflect the same philosophy: effective, considered, and never more than what your skin truly needs.

Our Philosophy

Three principles that guide everything we do

01

Natural Beauty

We enhance what is already there. Every treatment works with your skin's own rhythms and character, never against them. The goal is never to mask — it is to reveal a version of you that feels true and radiant.

02

Considered Space

From the bare plaster walls to the curated artwork, every detail of the studio is designed to slow things down. This is a space where you can breathe, disconnect, and trust the process.

03

Bespoke Care

No menu treatments here. Every appointment begins with a proper consultation, and every product and technique is chosen specifically for your skin on that day. It is beauty, tailored.

The Art

Bold faces on the walls

The studio walls are home to bold, colourful face paintings — vivid greens, yellows, and expressive brushwork that catch you the moment you walk in. They are a reminder that beauty at Ansikt is not about playing it safe. It is editorial, confident, and a little unexpected. The art reflects the brand: rooted in care, but never afraid of colour.
