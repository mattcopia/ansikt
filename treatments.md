- **Price:** £42
- **Patch test:** 48 hours required for new clients
- **Not suitable** for pregnancy and breastfeeding
- **Lasts:** 4-6 weeks

Together with a trained HD BrowSculpt specialist, we will discuss your desired brow goal whether it be simply natural or bold look. Brow lam is a two step treatment that involves correcting the brow by setting the hairs into a desired, long lasting shape. **PRE:** Complete client form and patch test at least 48 hours before appointment. For a maximum result, embrace the wild and let your brows grow. Come as you are, no need to remove make up - we simply tend to the brow area.

**POST:** Avoid getting your brows wet or touching them for 24 hours. Avoid sun exposure or direct heat such as sunbeds, saunas or steam rooms for 24 hours. We advise to invest in castor oil to nourish and condition brows between appointments. Speak to us for recommendations.
